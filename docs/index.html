SOP for Tree Core Sampling and Measurement for Dendroecology Analysis

I worked on this SOP during a research internship at the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Western Ecology Division in Corvallis, Oregon. Our research team looked at temperate forests in the Pacific Northwest across varying locations, topographies, elevations, and soil properties to determine seasonal factors that affect tree growth. My work primarily involved collecting tree cores and recording tree ring widths using WinDENDRO™ software. The full SOP included standardized methods to be used by qualified EPA staff for the acquisition and processing of tree core samples. The document linked below is an excerpt from the SOP of the section I wrote on analyzing tree cores with WinDENDRO™ software. I created this document in Microsoft Word and I have revised the content for clarity, grammar, and formatting.
